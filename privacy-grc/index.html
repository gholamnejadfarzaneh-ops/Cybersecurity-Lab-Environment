Automated GRC & Privacy Engineering

Operationalizing PIPEDA and GDPR Compliance through Infrastructure-as-Code

In modern enterprise environments, manual compliance is a bottleneck. This project demonstrates a Continuous Compliance approach, where privacy controls are embedded directly into the deployment pipeline (Privacy-by-Design).

Data Discovery

Automated scanning of databases to identify PII (Personally Identifiable Information) and apply tags for regulatory scope.

Access Control

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) enforced via Zero Trust policies, ensuring the 'Need-to-Know' principle.

Automated Audit

Real-time logging of all administrative actions to an immutable ledger for SOC2/ISO 27001 readiness.

Data Sovereignty

Geo-fencing policies to ensure Canadian data stays within local jurisdictions per PIPEDA requirements.

Project Impact

By automating the Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) workflow, we reduced the compliance review cycle from weeks to hours. This ensures that security teams are enablers of innovation rather than barriers.
